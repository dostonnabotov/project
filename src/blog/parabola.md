## Four types of parabola

  - Opening **up** side
  - Opening **down** side
  - Opening **left** side
  - Opening **right** side

### Up sided parabola

**Canonical Equation:** $ x^{2} = 2py $

**Coordinates of the focus:** $ F(\frac{p}{2}, 0) $

**Equation of the Directrix:** $ y = - \frac{p}{2} $
